Our Services

Electrical Maintenance & Minor Repairs

  • Fixture Installation: Replacing old ceiling lights, chandeliers, or wall sconces with new models
  • Ceiling Fan Installation: Swapping out existing fans or installing new ones in pre-wired locations.
  • Outlet & Switch Replacement: Upgrading old outlets to modern styles or replacing faulty light switches.
  • GFCI Outlet replacement: Enhancing safety in kitchens and bathrooms by installing Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter outlets.
  • Dimmer Switch Upgrades: Installing dimmer switches to help clients control ambiance and save energy.
  • Smoke & Carbon Monoxide Detectors: Replacing batteries or installing new battery-operated units.

Low-Voltage & Smart Home Services

  • Doorbell Installation: Setting up Ring, Nest, or other video doorbells.
  • Security Camera Setup: Installing plug-in or battery-operated outdoor/indoor security cameras.
  • Smart Home Integration: Setting up smart bulbs, plugs, and hubs.
  • TV Wall Mounting: Mounting TVs and using cable management kits to hide wires.
  • Landscape Lighting: Installing low-voltage (plug-in transformer-based) garden and pathway lights.

Plumbing Services

  • Faucet Repair & Replacement: Installing new kitchen or bathroom faucets and fixing leaks.
  • Toilet Repair & Installation: Replacing wax rings, flappers, fill valves, or installing a completely new toilet in an existing location.
  • Showerhead & Trim Replacement: Swapping out old showerheads or tub spouts for new ones
  • Minor clog Removal: Unclogging sinks, toilets, and tubs using standard mechanical tools.
  • Garbage Disposal Installation: Replacing an old unit with a new one in the same spot.
  • Sink Installation: Replacing a drop-in or undermount sink without moving the drain lines.
  • Hose Bib Repair: Replacing external outdoor faucets.
  • Leak Repair: Fixing minor leaks under sinks or in visible supply lines.

General Home Repairs & Maintenance

  • Drywall Repair & Patching: Expert repair of holes, cracks, and ceiling damage, including professional finishing and texture matching.
  • Door & Window Services: Installation of new door locks, deadbolts, and hardware, as well as screen repairs and door adjustments.
  • Mounting & Installation: Secure wall mounting for TVs, heavy mirrors, artwork, shelving, and curtain rods.
  • Furniture Assembly: Fast and correct assembly of all home and office furniture (IKEA, Wayfair, etc.).
  • Carpentry Finishes: Installation and repair of baseboards, crown molding, and interior trim work.
  • Cabinetry Upgrades: Replacing cabinet hinges, handles, and knobs to refresh your kitchen or bathroom.
  • Caulking & Grouting: Professional sealing of tubs, showers, and sinks to prevent water damage, and grout touch-ups for tile.
  • Exterior Maintenance: Pressure washing of driveways and decks, gutter cleaning, and minor fence or gate repairs.
  • Seasonal Tasks: Weatherproofing, insulation checks, and preparation for winter or summer conditions.
  • Deck & Porch Care: Staining, sealing, and replacing damaged boards to extend the life of your outdoor spaces.

Note: Handy Rafy LLC is a professional handyman service. We are not licensed plumbers or electricians. Our services are limited to minor repairs, maintenance, and "remove-and-replace" tasks. In compliance with Georgia law, we do not perform new installations or projects exceeding $2,500.
