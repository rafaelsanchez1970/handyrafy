Our Services

Electrical Maintenance & Minor Repairs

  • Installation: Replacing old ceiling lights with new models.
  • Ceiling Fan Installation: Swapping out existing fans or installing new ones.
  • Outlet & Switch Replacement: Upgrading old outlets or replacing faulty switches.
  • GFCI Outlet replacement: Enhancing safety in kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Dimmer Switch Upgrades: Installing dimmer switches for better ambiance.
  • Smoke & Carbon Monoxide Detectors: Battery replacement or new unit setup.

Low-Voltage & Smart Home Services

  • Doorbell Installation: Setting up Ring, Nest, or video doorbells.
  • Security Camera Setup: Installing outdoor/indoor security cameras.
  • Smart Home Integration: Setting up smart bulbs, plugs, and hubs.
  • TV Wall Mounting: Mounting TVs with cable management.
  • Landscape Lighting: Installing low-voltage garden lights.

Plumbing Services

  • Faucet Repair & Replacement: Installing new faucets and fixing leaks.
  • Toilet Repair & Installation: Replacing rings, valves, or complete units.
  • Showerhead & Trim: Swapping out old fixtures for new ones.
  • Minor Clog Removal: Unclogging sinks, toilets, and tubs.
  • Garbage Disposal: Replacing units in the same location.
  • Leak Repair: Fixing minor leaks in supply lines.

General Home Repairs & Maintenance

  • Drywall Repair: Patching holes and texture matching.
  • Door & Window: Locks, deadbolts, and screen repairs.
  • Mounting: Heavy mirrors, artwork, and shelving.
  • Furniture Assembly: IKEA, Wayfair, and office furniture.
  • Carpentry: Baseboards, crown molding, and trim.
  • Exterior: Pressure washing and gutter cleaning.

Note: Handy Rafy LLC is a professional handyman service. We are not licensed plumbers or electricians. Our services are limited to minor repairs, maintenance, and "remove-and-replace" tasks. In compliance with Georgia law, we do not perform new installations or projects exceeding $2,500.
