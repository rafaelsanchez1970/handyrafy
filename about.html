Who We Are

Welcome to Handy Rafy LLC, your reliable partner for home maintenance and repairs in Marietta and the surrounding areas. We pride ourselves on providing high-quality, professional handyman services for those smaller projects that keep your home running smoothly.

Legal Disclaimer & Licensing Information: In compliance with Georgia State Regulations, please be advised that Handy Rafy LLC is a professional handyman service provider and is not a state-licensed residential or general contractor.

We specialize in repair, maintenance, and improvement projects where the total value of labor and materials is under $2,500. We do not perform work involving structural changes, major electrical/plumbing systems, or HVAC services that require a specialized state license. We are fully registered to do business in the State of Georgia and carry the necessary City of Marietta local permits to operate in our community.
